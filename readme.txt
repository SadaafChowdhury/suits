It is a Suit e-commerce Website where we can customise our suit and order to be delivered to our doorsteps. Users can register
with their email, name, password, number and address and login with their email and password. Users will be redirected to the
home page, they can navigate to the product page, about page and cart. When users go to the product page they will select fabric,
size and shape and then order for home delivery. Admin can see the orderlist, users who have registered and they can add products
to the product page by including the picture, fabric name, description and price. The users can see their own order. Orderlist
contains address and number of the person to identify the customer along with fabric name, size  and shape.
